üìñ AI Glossary

View all terms ‚Üí

Chain-of-thought (C-o-T)

A prompting technique that encourages the AI to break down complex problems into a series of intermediate logical steps.

Context Grounding

Linking AI responses to specific, factual data to reduce hallucinations by forcing answers based only on provided information.

Balanced Prompting

Providing sufficient context and parameters while maintaining flexibility for creative or comprehensive responses.

Delimiters

Specific characters or symbols used to separate different parts of a prompt, helping AI distinguish instructions from data.

Context Window

The maximum amount of information an AI can hold in active memory during a single conversation.

Bias

The tendency of AI to produce slanted or unfair results based on prejudices found in its training data.

Burstiness

A measure of variation in sentence length. Human writing has natural mix; AI writing is often more uniform.

AI Response Analysis

The practice of reviewing AI output to ensure accuracy, lack of bias, and appropriate tone before use.

Deep Research Structuring

Organizing complex queries to guide AI through comprehensive data gathering and analysis.
